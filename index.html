What is a Knowledge Pack?

Knowledge Packs include both domain corpus (laws, codes, case law, templates) and profession-tuned AI (fine-tuned embeddings, reranking, and workflows). Your documents are automatically cross-referenced against the knowledge base, linking your work to relevant laws, regulations, and standards.

You're not locked in. Add your own files, books, contracts, or any knowledge you own—indexed, queryable, and citable alongside the Pack.

Same hardware. Same software. Different Knowledge Pack. That's how we scale from Legal to Medical to HR across Romania, Italy, Spain, and beyond.

Killer Workflow: Contract Variance Analysis

Today: A lawyer receives a 5-contract due diligence package. Manually read each (4+ hours), track differences in spreadsheets. Many solos decline these engagements.

With Inteligentia: Drag-drop the 5 PDFs. Query: "Show all indemnity clause variants with differences highlighted." Result in 2-3 minutes with citations.

This isn't about saving 4 hours. It's about taking the EUR 5,000+ engagement you'd otherwise turn down.
