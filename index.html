FAQ

Common Questions

How is my data protected?

All data on the device is encrypted at rest (full-disk encryption). There are no network interfaces - no Wiâ€‘Fi, Bluetooth, or Ethernet. Data transfer happens only via USBâ€‘C when you connect the device to your laptop.

What happens if the device breaks?

Hardware is covered by a 2-year warranty. Your documents remain on your computer - the device processes them but doesn't require you to transfer files permanently. If needed, encrypted backups can be restored to a replacement unit.

Do I need IT support to use this?

No. Setup takes under 5 minutes: connect USB-C to your laptop, plug in the power adapter, set your password. The companion app runs on Windows, Mac, and Linux with no installation required.

How do updates work without internet?

Updates are downloaded by the companion app (when you're online), cryptographically verified, and then transferred to the device over USB. The device itself never touches the internet.

What if I stop updating after the included period?

The device continues working on its last installed version. No remote lockout, no kill switch. You own it. Updates are optional value-adds, not hostage mechanisms.

What languages does it support?

The AI models support Romanian, Italian, Spanish, French, German, and English. Knowledge Packs are jurisdiction-specific - we're starting with Romanian legal, then expanding to Italy and other Romance-language markets.
