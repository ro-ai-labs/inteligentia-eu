Security & Privacy

Air-Gapped by Design

Inteligentia is built from the ground up for professionals who handle confidential information.
No cloud. No telemetry. Privacy by architecture.

Hardware Security

Privacy starts with the hardware. You can't leak data through a connection that doesn't exist.

No Radio Hardware

The Inteligentia appliance has no Wi-Fi, no Bluetooth, no cellular, no Ethernet. We use strict "No-Radio SKUs" from our supplier and verify each device has no M.2 WiFi module installed.

This isn't software-disabled networking. The radio hardware physically does not exist in the device.

USB-C Data Connectivity

The only external interface is USB-C, used for:

  • Storage mounting (your documents in, results out)
  • Companion app communication
  • Secure updates

Power is delivered via a separate DC barrel connector - the device cannot be powered over USB to prevent certain attack vectors.

Sealed Enclosure

Hardware is manufactured in Asia, but the "Brain" is flashed and the enclosure is physically sealed in Romania under our direct supervision. This provides chain-of-custody assurance.

Data Protection

Your documents never leave your desk. Here's how we ensure that.

LUKS Encryption at Rest

All user data is stored on a LUKS-encrypted partition. The encryption key is derived from your password and protected by the TPM (Trusted Platform Module).

This means your data is encrypted even if someone physically removes the storage drive.

Trust On First Use (TOFU)

When you first plug in your Inteligentia device, you create a master password. This establishes you as the device owner - no account creation, no email verification, no cloud authentication.

The first user becomes the owner. This is the same security model used in secure communication apps like Signal.

The Duress Protocol

For high-security situations, Inteligentia supports a duress password feature:

  • Master Password: Decrypts and boots normally
  • Duress Password: Instantly overwrites the LUKS header with random noise

If you're ever forced to unlock your device, entering the duress password makes all data permanently unrecoverable - it appears as a hardware failure.

Zero Telemetry

No network hardware means no data collection capability.

No Outbound Connections

The appliance never initiates outbound connections. It has no network stack, no DNS resolution, no ability to "phone home."

Updates are fetched by the companion app on your computer (when you're online), cryptographically verified, and then pushed to the device over USB. The device itself never touches the internet.

No Usage Analytics

We don't track:

  • What documents you upload
  • What questions you ask
  • How often you use the device
  • Any usage patterns whatsoever

This is enforced by hardware architecture, not just policy.

If the device works in airplane mode, your data stayed on your desk.

Secure Updates

Keeping your device current without compromising security.

Signed Updates via USB

Software and Knowledge Pack updates follow a secure process:

  • Updates are signed with our private key
  • Your companion app downloads and verifies the signature
  • Updates are transferred to the device over USB
  • The device verifies the signature again before applying

A compromised network cannot push malicious updates because the device never connects to a network.

No Kill Switch

If you decline updates, the device continues to function on its last installed version. There's no activation server, no license check, no remote lockout.

You own the device and the intelligence it contains.

Compliance Posture

Designed to simplify your regulatory obligations.

Built for GDPR-Aligned Workflows

By keeping all data processing local and eliminating data transfers:

  • No third-party data processors to manage
  • No data transfer impact assessments needed
  • No cloud provider DPAs to negotiate
  • Data stays in your physical possession

Note: Inteligentia is a tool designed to support compliance. Your specific regulatory obligations depend on your use case and jurisdiction.

Audit-Ready Architecture

Our deterministic build process and hash verification allow security auditors to:

  • Verify the exact software running on any device
  • Confirm no unauthorized modifications
  • Review the update chain of custody
